Step 1 Opener

"[Name], it's Owen from BackIn5. Reason I'm calling is, when I speak to [trade] firms doing emergency work, it's nearly always the same three things costing them money. I'm assuming at least one of these rings true for you, missed calls while you're on the tools, Checkatrade and directory leads going to whoever responds first, or half a day lost to someone just fishing for quotes. Which one's the real one for you?"

Which pain did they pick?

Step 2 Quantify the pain

Say this in sequence

"So that's X a week. Over a month that's £X. Over the year, £X you've handed to someone else." Pause. Don't fill it.

"So just to put that back to you, in a year of you being flat out busy, that's still [£X] walking out the door to whoever picked up the phone faster, or a lead you paid for going nowhere. Is that a position you're alright with, or does that need sorting?"

What did they say?

Detour Reopen the pain

Say this

"Sounds like it's not too bad then? Or does it get worse when you're properly slammed?"

What did they say?

End call Wrap up cleanly

Say this, then end the call

"Fair enough, sounds like it's not really biting for you at the moment. No harm, I'll leave you to it, appreciate you taking the call."

Step 3 Solution & pre-close

Say this in sequence

"This is exactly what BackIn5 sorts. Every enquiry, whatever comes in, wherever it's from, lands in one place, and gets an instant reply and follow-up even when you can't get to the phone. Think of it like having someone permanently answering for you, without hiring anyone."

"This closes exactly the gap we talked about. If it works like I've just described, would you actually want that running in your business?" Stop talking. Let them answer.

If they hesitate, want to see it first

"Fair, and if on seeing it, it's exactly what we've talked about, would you move forward?"

What did they say?

End call Wrap up cleanly

Say this, then end the call

"Fair enough, sounds like it's not the right fit or the right time. No harm, appreciate you talking it through, I'll leave it there."

Book it Close the call

Say this

"Good, then it's worth 3 minutes on WhatsApp video so you can see it live, not just hear me describe it. If you like it, I'll set you up on a 7 day trial. Morning or afternoon tomorrow?"

If hesitation on timing

"Worst case, you take a look and see how to solve it. Best case, we eliminate that issue and you make more money. Morning or afternoon?"
