Pre-call filter, dial only if 2 of 3 true

07 mobile as main contact
Solo or near-solo operator
Urgent job type

Objections, tap to pull up the response

Opener

"[Name], it's Owen from BackIn5. Quick reason I'm calling. When I speak to [trade] firms around [area], it's nearly always the same three things costing them money. Missed calls while you're on the tools, by the time you ring back the customer's already booked someone else. Checkatrade or directory leads going to whoever responds first. And time waster jobs, half a day going back and forth with someone who never had a budget. Which one of those rings true for you? And which would be the main one?"

Which pain did they pick?

Reveal gate, same for all three

"Has that actually cost you a job you know of, or just a feeling it happens?"

What did they say?

End the call here

"Fair enough, sounds like it's not really biting for you at the moment. No harm, I'll leave you to it, appreciate you taking the call."

Log as gate fail. Don't push into the maths, it won't hold. Move to the next number.

Quantify, gate passed

"How often is that happening, week or month?"

The maths, say slow, let it land

"So that's X a week. Over a month that's £X. Over the year, £X you've handed to someone else." Pause. Don't fill it.

"How long has that been going on?" Multiply monthly × 12, say it back. "And have you done anything to try and fix it?"

Have they tried anything already?

"If you had something that fixed all three, would that be worth looking into?"

"Tell you what, rather than fire you a link, I can show you the board in 3 minutes on WhatsApp video. If you like it, I'll send a 7 day trial. Morning or afternoon tomorrow?"

"Worst case, you take a look and see how to solve it. Best case, we eliminate that issue and you make more money. Morning or afternoon?"
