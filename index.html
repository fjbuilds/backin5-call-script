Pre-call filter

Trade business, active jobs on Google
Page 2+ or no web / FB presence
Reviews present (work is good)
Voicemail Under 25 seconds

"[Name], Owen at Tandemm. If I needed a [trade] near me, I'd have to scroll past 30 or 40 businesses to find you. I built you a website mockup, bring you 3 to 5 more customers a week. That's the foundation, not everything I do, but it's where it starts. If you're taking this business serious, shoot me a call or text back, I'll show you what we can do."

Don't leave this VM unless you've actually got a mockup ready. The hook is what earns the callback.

Objections, tap to pull up the response

Step 1 Opener

Pick the branch

"[Name]?... [Name], this is Owen, over at Tandemm. You on the job right now?"

Wait for answer.

"Not going to waste your time. I don't normally do this, I'm sure you've had calls like this before."

Confirmed on job / free to talk?

"[Name], this is Owen, over at Tandemm. You on the job right now?"

Wait for answer.

"Not going to waste your time. I don't normally do this. You're on page [X] of Google, great reviews, but have you got a website or Facebook page bringing that in?"

Confirmed on job / free to talk?

Step 2 Qualify — growth intent

"Are you looking to grow and scale the business right now?"

What did they say?

Detour Reflect the challenge

"You're not looking to grow the business and make more money?"

Did that reopen it?

Step 3 Diagnose the gap

"Could be your lucky day."

"I had to go to the third page to find you on Google. Good reviews, but I can't see your work. How long have you been in business for?"

Let them answer.

"You've been in business a while. Why don't you have a social presence that's bringing work in for you?"

Let them answer. Don't fill the silence.

Once they've answered

Step 4 Quantify & roll-up

Say this in sequence

"Could you handle 3 to 5 new customers a week?"

Capacity check. If they push back on 3 to 5, get a real number.

"If I'm a customer, why would I scroll to page 3 and go with you, instead of your competitors on page 1, who've got a presence, pictures, the lot?"

"So right now, every one of those 3 to 5 jobs a week is going to someone else on page 1. Not because they do better work than you, because they're the one being seen. Over a year, that's a lot of jobs you never even got a shot at." Pause. Don't fill it.

What did they say?

Detour Reopen the pain

"Sounds like it's not too bad then? Or does it sting more when you think about what your competitors are picking up instead?"

What did they say?

Step 6 Solution & pre-close

Say this in sequence

"This is exactly what Tandemm sorts. We get you found first, page one, proper presence, work that actually shows what you can do, then we send the leads it brings in straight to you, tracked, so nothing gets missed."

"If that means 3 to 5 more jobs a week landing with you instead of your competitors, is that something you'd want running?" Stop talking. Let them answer.

If they hesitate or want proof first

"Fair, and if I show you exactly where you're losing those jobs to right now, would you move on it?"

What did they say?

Book it Close — book the audit

"Good. I'll run a proper audit on where you're losing out, your ranking, your competitors, what it'd take to fix it. Takes 10 minutes to walk you through it. Morning or afternoon tomorrow?"

If hesitation on timing

"Worst case, you see exactly what's costing you the work. Best case, we fix it and those jobs start landing with you. Morning or afternoon?"

Exit 0 Bad time

"No worries, I'll catch you another time. Appreciate it."

Exit 1 No growth intent

"Fair enough, sounds like it's not the priority right now. I'll leave you to it, appreciate you taking the call."

Exit 2 Pain didn't land

"Fair enough, sounds like it's not really biting for you at the moment. No harm, I'll leave you to it."

Exit 3 Pre-close no

"Fair enough, sounds like it's not the right fit or the right time. Appreciate you talking it through, I'll leave it there."
